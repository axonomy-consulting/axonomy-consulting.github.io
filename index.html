Our Services.

Systems architecture

For more than a decade, we have been developing service-oriented architecture, content architecture and high availability designs for a wide range of businesses in several industries.

Enterpise Architecture.

Axonomy enterprise architects develop entire technology strategies for large organisations. Our consultants take responsibility for the high-level planning and coordination that solution architects rely on.

We create conceptual blueprints for the operation and structure of business IT systems. We assess the specific goals and future objectives of organisations, and then set about designing systems architecture that delivers them.

Solution Architecture.

Axonomy solution architects manage the many applications and infrastructure inter-dependencies of large organisations. From conception to delivery, our consultants ensure the technical consistency and integrity of specific projects.

Our consultants make technical decisions to create solutions for specific IT goals and objectives. We work with our clients to create solutions that increase productivity, reduce costs, streamline day-to-day business activities and maximise system security.
